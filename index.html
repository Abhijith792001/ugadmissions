Instructions

  • This is the only portal to apply for any UG programmes (other than B.Tech) offered by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. / external portal to collect applications.
  • We have no agents offering admissions for any programmes, at any campuses. University will not be responsible for any such acts if the candidates fall victim of such agents/third parties.
  • Selection to B.Sc. Nursing, B.Sc. (Hons.) in Agriculture, B.Sc. Allied Health Sciences, and B.Pharm is based on the Amrita Entrance Examination – Life & Health Sciences.
  • Selection to all the other programmes is based on the marks scored in the core subjects in +2 and an interview.
  • Select the courses of your choice in the next screen and complete the payment.
  • Updating the marks, documents etc. will be activated only on a later stage.
